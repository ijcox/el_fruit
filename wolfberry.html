Wolfberry

Wolfberry (Lycium barbarum), also known as goji berry, is a bright red-orange fruit widely used in traditional Asian medicine and cuisine.

Description

The fruit is small, elongated, and bright red when ripe, with a slightly sweet and tangy flavor when dried or fresh.

Distribution and Habitat

  • China (origin)
  • Tibet
  • Mongolia
  • Temperate and dry regions

Cultivation

  • Dry temperate climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Hardy shrub plant

Uses

  • Dried snacks
  • Teas and soups
  • Traditional herbal medicine

Interesting Facts

  • Commonly marketed as a “superfood.”
  • Used in Chinese medicine for thousands of years.
