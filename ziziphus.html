Ziziphus

Ziziphus is a genus of fruit-bearing shrubs and trees, including jujube, known for sweet edible fruits and medicinal uses.

Description

Fruits are small, round to oval, turning from green to reddish-brown when ripe, with sweet crisp or chewy flesh.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Asia
  • Africa
  • Middle East
  • Dry and subtropical regions

Cultivation

  • Hot dry climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Hardy drought-resistant trees

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Dried fruit snacks
  • Traditional medicine

Interesting Facts

  • Includes the popular jujube fruit.
  • Extremely drought tolerant species.
