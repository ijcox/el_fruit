Monstera Deliciosa

Monstera deliciosa is a tropical plant that produces an edible fruit known for its unique pineapple-banana flavor when fully ripe.

Description

The fruit is long and green with hexagonal scales. As it ripens, the outer scales fall away, revealing sweet, creamy edible flesh underneath.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Central America
  • Mexico
  • Tropical rainforests

Cultivation

  • Warm tropical climates
  • High humidity
  • Partial shade
  • Support structures for climbing growth

Uses

  • Fresh eating (when fully ripe)
  • Ornamental plant
  • Tropical gardening

Interesting Facts

  • Unripe fruit contains irritating compounds and must not be eaten.
  • The plant is also famous as a houseplant.
