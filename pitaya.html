Pitaya

Pitaya, commonly known as dragon fruit, is a striking tropical cactus fruit known for its vibrant appearance and refreshing, mildly sweet taste.

Description

The fruit has bright pink or yellow skin with scale-like “flames,” and white or red flesh dotted with tiny edible black seeds.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Central America (origin)
  • South America
  • Southeast Asia
  • Tropical and subtropical regions

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained sandy soil
  • Climbing cactus plant

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Smoothies and juices
  • Fruit bowls and desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Grown on a night-blooming cactus.
  • Flowers are large, fragrant, and bloom only at night.
