Lime

Lime (Citrus aurantiifolia and related species) is a small citrus fruit known for its tart flavor and bright green color.

Description

The fruit is round to oval, with thin green skin and juicy, acidic pulp. It is widely used for its refreshing and zesty taste.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Tropical regions worldwide
  • Mexico
  • India
  • Caribbean

Cultivation

  • Tropical and subtropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Warm temperatures

Uses

  • Cooking and flavoring
  • Beverages
  • Marinades
  • Desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Used extensively in Mexican and Southeast Asian cuisines.
  • Highly valued for its refreshing acidity.
