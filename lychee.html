Lychee

Lychee (Litchi chinensis) is a tropical fruit known for its sweet, floral flavor and juicy translucent flesh.

Description

The fruit is small and round with rough, reddish skin that peels easily to reveal soft white flesh surrounding a single dark seed.

Distribution and Habitat

  • China (origin)
  • India
  • Thailand
  • Vietnam

Cultivation

  • Tropical and subtropical climates
  • High humidity
  • Well-drained soil
  • Warm temperatures

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Desserts and fruit salads
  • Juices and beverages

Interesting Facts

  • Cultivated for over 2,000 years.
  • Highly valued in Chinese culture and cuisine.
