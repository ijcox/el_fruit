Java Apple

Java apple (Syzygium samarangense), also known as wax apple, is a tropical fruit with a crisp, watery texture and mild sweetness.

Description

The fruit is bell-shaped, shiny, and typically red, pink, or green with very juicy, airy flesh and a subtle floral flavor.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Southeast Asia
  • India
  • Pacific Islands

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • High humidity
  • Full sun
  • Frequent watering

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Fruit salads
  • Juices and desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Contains very high water content.
  • Often served chilled in tropical regions.
