Olive

Olive (Olea europaea) is a small fruit known for its rich oil content and central role in Mediterranean cuisine and culture.

Description

The fruit is small and oval, turning from green to black as it ripens, with a bitter raw taste that becomes edible after curing.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Mediterranean region
  • Middle East
  • California
  • Australia

Cultivation

  • Hot, dry climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained rocky soil
  • Drought tolerant trees

Uses

  • Olive oil production
  • Table olives
  • Culinary uses

Interesting Facts

  • One of the oldest cultivated trees in history.
  • Olive branches symbolize peace.
