Grape

Grapes are small, sweet berries that grow in clusters on vines of the genus Vitis. They are one of the world’s most widely cultivated fruits.

Description

Grapes can be green, red, purple, or black. They are juicy, soft-skinned berries with one or more seeds depending on the variety.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Europe
  • North America
  • South America
  • Asia

Cultivation

  • Warm climates
  • Well-drained soil
  • Full sun
  • Support structures (vines)

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Wine production
  • Juice and raisins

Interesting Facts

  • One of the oldest cultivated fruits.
  • Used in winemaking for thousands of years.
