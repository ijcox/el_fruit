Indian Plum

Indian plum (Flacourtia jangomas), also known as coffee plum, is a small tropical fruit tree producing edible dark purple fruits.

Description

The fruit is round, turning red to dark purple when ripe. It has soft, juicy flesh with a mildly sweet and tangy flavor.

Distribution and Habitat

  • India
  • Southeast Asia
  • South Asia

Cultivation

  • Tropical and subtropical climates
  • Moist, well-drained soil
  • Full sun
  • Drought tolerant once established

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Jams and preserves
  • Traditional food use

Interesting Facts

  • The tree is often used as a hedge or boundary plant.
  • Fruit is popular in rural areas but less known globally.
