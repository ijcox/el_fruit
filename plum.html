Plum

Plum (Prunus domestica and related species) is a sweet or tart stone fruit known for its juicy flesh and smooth skin.

Description

The fruit is round or oval with smooth skin ranging from purple, red, yellow, or green, and a single hard pit inside.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Europe
  • Asia
  • North America
  • Temperate regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Temperate climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained fertile soil
  • Cold winter required

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Jams and preserves
  • Baking and desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Plums can be eaten fresh or dried into prunes.
  • There are hundreds of cultivated varieties worldwide.
