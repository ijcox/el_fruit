Greengage

Greengage is a type of plum known for its sweet, aromatic flavor and greenish-yellow skin even when ripe.

Description

Greengages are small, round plums with smooth skin and very juicy, honey-like flesh.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Europe
  • United Kingdom
  • Western Asia

Cultivation

  • Temperate climates
  • Well-drained soil
  • Full sun

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Jams and preserves
  • Baking

Interesting Facts

  • Highly prized in European cuisine.
  • Considered one of the sweetest plum varieties.
