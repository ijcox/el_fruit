Grumichama

Grumichama is a tropical fruit tree native to South America, producing small dark purple fruits similar to cherries.

Description

The fruit is round, dark purple to black when ripe, with juicy sweet pulp surrounding a seed.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Brazil
  • Paraguay
  • Uruguay

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Moist soil
  • Partial shade to full sun

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Jams
  • Ornamental planting

Interesting Facts

  • Related to Surinam cherry.
  • Fruit tastes like a sweet cherry with tropical notes.
