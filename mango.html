Mango

Mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical stone fruit known as the “king of fruits” for its rich sweetness and global popularity.

Description

The fruit is oval with smooth skin ranging from green to yellow, orange, or red, containing juicy, fragrant flesh surrounding a large flat seed.

Distribution and Habitat

  • India (origin)
  • Thailand
  • Philippines
  • Latin America

Cultivation

  • Tropical and subtropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Warm temperatures

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Smoothies and juices
  • Desserts and chutneys

Interesting Facts

  • One of the most widely cultivated fruits in the world.
  • There are hundreds of mango varieties.
