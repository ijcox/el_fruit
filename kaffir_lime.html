Kaffir Lime

Kaffir lime (Citrus hystrix) is a citrus fruit known for its highly aromatic leaves and bumpy green rind.

Description

The fruit is small, round, and dark green with a thick, wrinkled peel. The juice is very sour, but the leaves are widely used in cooking.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Southeast Asia
  • Thailand
  • Indonesia
  • Malaysia

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Moderate watering

Uses

  • Culinary leaves (Thai cuisine)
  • Zest and peel flavoring
  • Traditional remedies

Interesting Facts

  • The leaves are more commonly used than the fruit itself.
  • Highly aromatic and used in soups and curries.
