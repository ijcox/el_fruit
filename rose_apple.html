Rose Apple

Rose apple (Syzygium jambos) is a tropical fruit known for its crisp texture, mild sweetness, and floral rose-like aroma.

Description

The fruit is bell-shaped or round with pale yellow, pink, or green skin and crunchy juicy flesh inside.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Southeast Asia (origin)
  • India
  • Caribbean
  • Tropical regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Moist well-drained soil
  • Evergreen tree

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Fruit salads
  • Jams and beverages

Interesting Facts

  • Its fragrance resembles rose petals.
  • Related to wax apple and java apple.
