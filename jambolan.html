Jambolan

Jambolan (Syzygium cumini), also known as Java plum, is a tropical fruit tree producing dark purple berries.

Description

The fruit is oval-shaped, turning dark purple or black when ripe, with juicy, slightly astringent flesh and a single seed.

Distribution and Habitat

  • India
  • Southeast Asia
  • Australia (naturalized)

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Moist soil
  • Full sun
  • Moderate rainfall regions

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Juices and wines
  • Traditional medicine

Interesting Facts

  • Seeds are used in traditional diabetes remedies.
  • The fruit stains the tongue deep purple.
