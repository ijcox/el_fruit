Huckleberry

Huckleberries are small wild berries in the genera Vaccinium and related plants, commonly found in North America.

Description

Small round berries that can be blue, purple, or black with a sweet-tart flavor similar to blueberries.

Distribution and Habitat

  • North America
  • Mountain forests

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Pies and desserts
  • Jams and syrups

Interesting Facts

  • Closely related to blueberries.
  • Important wild food source for wildlife and humans.
