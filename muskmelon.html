Muskmelon

Muskmelon (Cucumis melo) is a sweet, aromatic melon known for its juicy orange or green flesh and netted rind varieties.

Description

The fruit is round to oval, with a rough netted or smooth rind depending on variety, and soft, fragrant, sweet flesh inside.

Distribution and Habitat

  • South Asia
  • Middle East
  • Mediterranean region
  • Worldwide cultivation

Cultivation

  • Warm climates
  • Full sun
  • Sandy, well-drained soil
  • Seasonal crop

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Fruit salads
  • Desserts and smoothies

Interesting Facts

  • Includes cantaloupe and other melon varieties.
  • Highly fragrant when ripe.
