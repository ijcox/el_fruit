Sapote

Sapote is a general name for several soft, sweet tropical fruits from different species, often known for their creamy texture and rich flavor.

Description

Sapote fruits vary widely but are typically soft, custard-like inside with colors ranging from orange to brown or green depending on the type.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Central America (origin)
  • Mexico
  • Caribbean
  • Tropical regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Warm tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained fertile soil
  • Evergreen trees

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Ice creams and smoothies
  • Desserts and puddings

Interesting Facts

  • “Sapote” refers to multiple unrelated fruit species.
  • Some varieties taste like pumpkin or chocolate.
