Strawberry Tree Fruit

Strawberry tree fruit (Arbutus unedo) is a Mediterranean fruit known for its round red appearance and mildly sweet, grainy pulp.

Description

The fruit is small, round, and red with a textured surface, resembling a strawberry but with a softer, less intense flavor.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Mediterranean region (native)
  • Western Europe
  • Ireland
  • Warm temperate climates

Cultivation

  • Mild climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained acidic soil
  • Evergreen shrub or small tree

Uses

  • Jams and preserves
  • Liqueurs and wines
  • Ornamental planting

Interesting Facts

  • Flowers and ripe fruit can appear at the same time.
  • Also called “kitchen fruit” in some regions due to limited raw consumption.
