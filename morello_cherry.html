Morello Cherry

Morello cherry is a sour cherry variety known for its deep red color and tart flavor, widely used in cooking and baking.

Description

The fruit is small, round, and dark red to almost black when ripe, with juicy but very tart flesh.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Europe
  • United Kingdom
  • United States (cultivated)

Cultivation

  • Temperate climates
  • Cold-hardy trees
  • Well-drained soil
  • Full sun

Uses

  • Pies and desserts
  • Jams and preserves
  • Cooking sauces
  • Cherry liqueurs

Interesting Facts

  • More tart than sweet cherry varieties.
  • Popular in traditional European baking.
