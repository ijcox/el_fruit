Longan

Longan (Dimocarpus longan) is a tropical fruit related to lychee, known for its sweet, translucent flesh and dark seed.

Description

The fruit is small and round with a thin brown shell. Inside is juicy, pale flesh surrounding a shiny black seed, giving it the nickname “dragon’s eye.”

Distribution and Habitat

  • China
  • Thailand
  • Vietnam
  • Taiwan

Cultivation

  • Tropical and subtropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Warm temperatures

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Dried fruit
  • Traditional Chinese desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Name means “dragon eye” in Chinese.
  • Often used in herbal soups and teas.
