Jujube

Jujube (Ziziphus jujuba) is a small sweet fruit also known as Chinese date, widely cultivated in Asia for thousands of years.

Description

The fruit is small and oval, starting green and turning reddish-brown when ripe, with a crisp apple-like texture when fresh and a chewy date-like texture when dried.

Distribution and Habitat

  • China
  • Korea
  • India
  • Mediterranean region

Cultivation

  • Dry to temperate climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Drought tolerant

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Dried fruit (like dates)
  • Herbal medicine
  • Teas and syrups

Interesting Facts

  • Cultivated for over 4,000 years.
  • Highly valued in traditional Chinese medicine.
