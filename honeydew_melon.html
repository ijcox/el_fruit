Honeydew Melon

Honeydew melon is a sweet, juicy fruit from the melon family known for its pale green flesh and smooth rind.

Description

Round to oval melon with a smooth pale yellow-green exterior and soft, sweet green flesh.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Worldwide cultivation
  • Warm climates

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Fruit salads
  • Smoothies

Interesting Facts

  • One of the most popular summer melons.
  • High water content makes it very hydrating.
