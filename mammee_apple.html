Mammee Apple

Mammee apple (Mammea americana) is a tropical fruit native to the Caribbean and Central America, valued for its sweet, aromatic flesh.

Description

The fruit is round with thick, brownish skin and bright orange flesh. It contains one to several large seeds and has a rich, slightly apricot-like flavor.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Caribbean
  • Central America
  • South America
  • Tropical regions

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Slow-growing tree

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Jams and preserves
  • Desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Not closely related to true apples.
  • The seeds and skin can be toxic and should not be eaten.
