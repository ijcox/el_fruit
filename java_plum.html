Java Plum

Java plum (Syzygium cumini), also known as jamun, is a tropical fruit known for its deep purple color and sweet-tart flavor.

Description

The fruit is oval-shaped and turns dark purple or black when ripe, with juicy flesh that can stain the tongue purple.

Distribution and Habitat

  • India
  • Southeast Asia
  • Australia (naturalized)

Cultivation

  • Tropical and subtropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Moist, well-drained soil
  • Moderate rainfall

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Juices and wines
  • Traditional medicine

Interesting Facts

  • Seeds are used in traditional remedies for blood sugar control.
  • The tree is long-living and highly adaptable.
