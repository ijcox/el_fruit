Vietnamese Guava

Vietnamese guava is a popular tropical guava variety known for its crisp texture, light sweetness, and refreshing aroma.

Description

The fruit is round or pear-shaped with light green to yellow skin and firm white or pink flesh containing many small seeds.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Vietnam (widely cultivated)
  • Southeast Asia
  • Tropical and subtropical regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Warm tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Fast-growing fruit tree

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Salads with salt/sugar dips
  • Juices and smoothies

Interesting Facts

  • Often eaten while still slightly unripe and crisp.
  • Highly valued street fruit in Southeast Asia.
