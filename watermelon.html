Watermelon

Watermelon (Citrullus lanatus) is a large, juicy fruit known for its refreshing sweetness and high water content, especially popular in warm climates.

Description

The fruit has a thick green rind and soft red, pink, or sometimes yellow flesh filled with black or white seeds.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Africa (origin)
  • Worldwide cultivation
  • Hot and dry climates
  • Tropical and subtropical regions

Cultivation

  • Warm climates
  • Full sun
  • Sandy well-drained soil
  • Vining annual plant

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Juices and smoothies
  • Fruit salads

Interesting Facts

  • Composed of over 90% water.
  • Some varieties are seedless.
