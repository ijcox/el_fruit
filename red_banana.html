Red Banana

Red banana is a colorful banana variety known for its reddish-purple peel and sweeter, creamier flesh than common yellow bananas.

Description

The fruit is shorter and thicker than standard bananas, with red to maroon skin and pale cream to pinkish flesh inside.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Southeast Asia
  • India
  • Central America
  • Tropical regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Warm temperatures
  • Rich, moist soil
  • Full sun

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Smoothies
  • Desserts and fruit salads

Interesting Facts

  • Often has berry-like or raspberry notes in flavor.
  • Popular in tropical markets worldwide.
