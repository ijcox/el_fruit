Honeyberry

Honeyberry (Lonicera caerulea) is a small blue berry known for its early ripening season and sweet-tart flavor.

Description

Elongated blue berries with soft skin and juicy interior, similar in taste to blueberries with a hint of raspberry.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Russia
  • Japan
  • Canada

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Jams
  • Freezing and baking

Interesting Facts

  • One of the earliest fruits to ripen in spring.
  • Very cold-hardy shrub.
