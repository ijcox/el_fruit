Star Fruit

Star fruit (Averrhoa carambola), also called carambola, is a tropical fruit known for its distinctive star shape when sliced.

Description

The fruit is yellow-green and crisp, with five ridges that form a perfect star shape in cross-section.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Southeast Asia (origin)
  • India
  • South America
  • Tropical and subtropical regions

Cultivation

  • Warm humid climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Evergreen fruit tree

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Juices and smoothies
  • Garnishes and salads

Interesting Facts

  • The entire fruit is edible, including the skin.
  • Can taste sweet or tart depending on ripeness.
