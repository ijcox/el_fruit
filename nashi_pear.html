Nashi Pear

Nashi pear (also known as Asian pear) is a crisp, juicy fruit that combines the texture of an apple with the flavor of a pear.

Description

The fruit is round and apple-shaped, with thin golden-brown or yellow skin and very crunchy, watery, sweet flesh.

Distribution and Habitat

  • China
  • Japan
  • Korea
  • Worldwide cultivation

Cultivation

  • Temperate climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained soil
  • Cold winter required for fruiting

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Fruit salads
  • Juices

Interesting Facts

  • Very high water content (extremely juicy).
  • Also called “apple pear” due to its shape and texture.
