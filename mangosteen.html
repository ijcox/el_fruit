Mangosteen

Mangosteen (Garcinia mangostana) is a tropical fruit famous for its sweet, juicy white segments and thick purple rind.

Description

The fruit is round with a deep purple outer shell and soft, snow-white segments inside that are sweet, slightly tangy, and very aromatic.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Indonesia
  • Thailand
  • Malaysia
  • Southeast Asia tropical regions

Cultivation

  • Tropical rainforest climate
  • High humidity
  • Rich, well-drained soil
  • Partial shade when young

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Desserts
  • Juices and supplements

Interesting Facts

  • Often called the “queen of fruits.”
  • Very slow-growing tree, taking years to bear fruit.
