Pineapple

Pineapple (Ananas comosus) is a tropical fruit known for its sweet, tangy flavor and spiky exterior.

Description

The fruit is a large compound fruit with tough, spiky skin and juicy yellow flesh made up of many fused berries.

Distribution and Habitat

  • South America (origin)
  • Hawaii
  • Philippines
  • Tropical regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Sandy, well-drained soil
  • Low-growing perennial plant

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Juices and smoothies
  • Cooking and desserts

Interesting Facts

  • Each pineapple is made up of many individual fruitlets.
  • Contains bromelain, an enzyme that breaks down protein.
