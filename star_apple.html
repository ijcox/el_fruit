Star Apple

Star apple (Chrysophyllum cainito) is a tropical fruit known for its sweet, milky pulp and star-shaped pattern inside when cut horizontally.

Description

The fruit is round with smooth purple or green skin and a soft, jelly-like interior that reveals a star pattern in cross-section.

Distribution and Habitat

  • Caribbean (origin)
  • Central America
  • South America
  • Tropical regions worldwide

Cultivation

  • Hot tropical climates
  • Full sun
  • Well-drained fertile soil
  • Evergreen fruit tree

Uses

  • Fresh eating
  • Fruit desserts
  • Juices and beverages

Interesting Facts

  • The cross-section resembles a star, giving it its name.
  • Has a mild, sweet, milky flavor similar to lychee.
